RULE-104:

National space activities A space activity is a "national space activity" of a given state if:

  1. it is carried on by a government agency of that state;
  2. it is carried on by a non-governmental entity which is national,either a natural or judicial,of that state,in accordance with a particular circumstances of each case under respective national legislation; or
  3. the activity has a territorial nexus with or is otherwise attributable to that state
